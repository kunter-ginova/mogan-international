Mogan International
Research Center

An international research platform advancing scientific inquiry across food systems, nutrition science, and global food security, anchored at Middle East Technical University.

Institutional Mandate

MOGAN-IRC operates as an international research center dedicated to generating high-integrity scientific knowledge, fostering cross-border collaboration, and informing policy through evidence-based research.

Research Domains

Food Systems

Production, processing, value chains, and resilience of global food systems.

Nutrition Science

Metabolic health, dietary patterns, and systems-level nutritional research.

Food Security

Strategic, geopolitical, and policy-driven approaches to food security.
