The Center

MIRC is built to produce outputs that are usable by institutions: not only publishable, but operationally adoptable. Governance and integrity are treated as first-class constraints.

Mission

  • Convert evidence and standards into decision artifacts.
  • Deliver training that changes professional practice, not vocabulary.
  • Support institutions facing food, nutrition, and risk complexity.
private center decision-grade deployable outputs

Operating Model

  • Program lines with defined deliverables and timelines.
  • Small-team execution with partner network scaling.
  • Transparency on sponsorship and conflicts of interest.
program-based networked ethics-first

Integrity Commitments

  • Clear separation between research, training, and advisory work.
  • Publication integrity and method disclosure when applicable.
  • Respect for confidentiality in institutional engagements.
COI disclosure confidentiality method clarity

Outputs
